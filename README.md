Life Assurance Retail Dashboard – Summary

This dashboard provides a high-level and drill-down view of Life Assurance Retail performance across Worksite and Bancassurance channels. It tracks key metrics such as total retail premium (₦214M), policies sold, average case size, and staff performance against targets.

Key insights include:

Risk vs Savings ratios by policy count and premium collected, showing portfolio mix and target alignment.

Average case size performance compared to targets for both distribution channels.

Staff performance breakdown into Laggard, Average, and Excellent categories, with drill-downs by agent.

Regional premium distribution, highlighting top-contributing regions such as Lagos and other Nigerian regions.

Year-on-year premium trend analysis, comparing current year performance against the previous year.

Channel comparison of premiums collected between Worksite and Bancassurance.

Overall, the dashboard supports performance monitoring, strategic decision-making, and operational insights across channels, staff, and regions.
